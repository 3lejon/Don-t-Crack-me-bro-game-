Don’t Cack Me Bro

Welcome to Don’t Cack Me Bro, a chaotic little rage platformer built in the spirit of old‑school flash games. It’s fast, it’s unfair, and it absolutely wants you to lose your cool — but in a fun way.
What to Expect

    Tight, punishing jumps that reward precision

    Original art drawn specifically for this project

    Retro flash‑game vibes with quick resets and simple controls

    Parallax backgrounds to give the world some extra depth
